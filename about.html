About Us

Company History

ELSADEB SERVICES LTD was founded in [year] by [founder's name] with a vision to make a positive impact on individuals and families within our community. What began as a small initiative has grown into a trusted institution known for its commitment to excellence and compassionate care. Over the years, we have served countless clients, providing a range of services aimed at enhancing mental wellness, nurturing young minds, and empowering individuals to lead fulfilling lives.

Mission

Our mission at ELSADEB SERVICES LTD is simple yet profound: to give meaning to life. We believe that every individual deserves to live a life filled with purpose, joy, and fulfillment. Through our diverse range of services and programs, we strive to support and empower our clients on their journey towards holistic wellness and personal growth. Whether through education, counselling, or mentoring, we are dedicated to helping individuals discover their strengths, overcome obstacles, and embrace their true potential.

Values

Compassion: We approach every interaction with empathy, understanding, and genuine care for the well-being of our clients.

Excellence: We are committed to delivering services of the highest quality, continuously striving for excellence in everything we do.

Empowerment: We believe in empowering individuals to take control of their lives, equipping them with the tools and resources needed to thrive.

Diversity and Inclusion: We celebrate diversity and champion inclusivity, respecting the unique backgrounds, experiences, and perspectives of all individuals.

Integrity: We uphold the highest standards of integrity, honesty, and ethical conduct in all aspects of our work.

Collaboration: We believe in the power of collaboration and teamwork, working closely with our clients and partners to achieve shared goals and objectives.

At ELSADEB SERVICES LTD, we are dedicated to making a meaningful difference in the lives of those we serve, guided by our mission to give meaning to life.

Meet our team

Profiles of the key team members or founders of ELSADEB SERVICES LTD

Dr. Chibuike Emmanuel Ikechukwu

Position: Founder, Chaplain, Mentor, and Life Coach

Qualifications: PhD (Divinity and Theology), BSc (Counselling and Psychotherapy), Dip.(Counselling studies)

Expertise: With a background in Divinity and Theology, coupled with extensive training in counselling and psychotherapy, Dr. Ikechukwu brings a unique blend of spiritual insight and therapeutic expertise to his role. As a chaplain, mentor, and life coach, he is dedicated to supporting individuals on their journey towards personal growth, emotional well-being, and spiritual fulfillment.

Sarah Emmanuel-Ikechukwu

Position: Co-Founder, Special Education Needs Officer (SENCO)

Qualifications: Holds a PGD & BSc in Early Years Education.

Expertise: With a background in early years education and special education needs, Sarah Emmanuel-Ikechukwu brings a wealth of experience to her role as co-founder of ELSADEB SERVICES LTD. As a seasoned educator and SENCO officer, she has successfully transformed nurseries and supported children with diverse learning needs. Her dedication to creating nurturing environments for young learners has earned her a reputation for excellence in the field.

Combined Experience

Successful Parents: Both Dr. Chibuike Emmanuel Ikechukwu and Sarah Emmanuel-Ikechukwu are not only accomplished professionals but also successful parents themselves. Drawing from their personal experiences in raising well-rounded children, they understand the challenges and joys of parenthood firsthand. This unique perspective informs their approach to supporting families and individuals through ELSADEB SERVICES LTD, ensuring that their services are grounded in empathy, understanding, and practical wisdom.
